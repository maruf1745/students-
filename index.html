About Us



Eagyleeye for School

Eagleye for Schools is a school and trip management system that allows schools to manage their students, staff, buses and trips. It also allows parents/students to track the school bus and trip activity through our Eagleeye for School mobile application. It gives the parents/students a real time trip tracking and notification about the trip status.
